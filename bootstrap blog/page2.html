Good Night (2023 film)

Good Night is a 2023 Indian Tamil-language romantic comedy[2] film written and directed by Vinayak Chandrasekaran, in his directorial debut, and jointly produced by Million Dollar Studios and MRP Entertainment. The film stars Manikandan, alongside Meetha Raghunath, Ramesh Thilak, Raichal Rabecca, Balaji Sakthivel and Bagavathi Perumal. It follows Mohan, an IT youngster, who has a snoring problem, which affects his life.

Vada Chennai

Vada Chennai is a 2018 Indian Tamil-language crime drama film[3] written and directed by Vetrimaaran in his fourth film as a director. It is the first instalment in a planned trilogy. Produced by A. Subaskaran's Lyca Productions and Dhanush's Wunderbar Films with Vetrimaaran's Grass Root Film Company, it stars Dhanush as Anbu, a skilled carrom player who becomes a reluctant participant in a gang war between two rival gangsters.

Pudhupettai

"Pudhupettai" is a 2006 Indian Tamil-language crime action film directed by Selvaraghavan, starring Dhanush in the lead role, Sonia Agarwal and Sneha in supporting roles. The movie follows the story of a slum-dwelling student from Pudhupettai who rises to become a dreaded gangster in North Chennai as a means of survival after witnessing his mother's murder.

Taramani

Taramani is a 2017 Indian Tamil-language drama film written and directed by Ram. He co-produced the film with J. Satish Kumar under his production company Catamaran Productions. The film stars Andrea Jeremiah, Vasanth Ravi and Adrian Knight Jesly, with Anjali appearing in a cameo. Kattradhu Thamizh

"Kattradhu Thamizh" is a 2007 Indian Tamil-language psychological thriller film directed by Ram and produced by Salmara Mohammad Sharief. The film stars Jiiva, Anjali, and Karunas.The story revolves around a young post-graduate who faces difficulties in finding employment and struggles against social inequalities.

Viduthalai Part 1

Viduthalai Part 1 is a 2023 Indian Tamil-language period crime thriller film directed by Vetrimaaran and produced by Elred Kumar under the banners of RS Infotainment. It is adapted from the short story Thunaivan by B. Jeyamohan. The film stars Soori, Vijay Sethupathi, Bhavani Sre, Gautham Vasudev Menon, Rajiv Menon and Ilavarasu. It follows a police constable who is recruited to arrest the leader of a separatist group.
